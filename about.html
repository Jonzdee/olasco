Our Mission

Engineering
Excellence from the
Heart of Nigeria

Olasco Engineering & Fabrication Ltd was founded in 2001 in Lagos with a single conviction: that Nigerian engineers and fabricators could meet — and exceed — international standards on home soil.

Over two decades later, we have completed over 420 projects across Nigeria's upstream, midstream, downstream, marine, and power sectors. From pipe spools on the Escravos Gas-to-Liquids Plant to structural steel packages for NLNG Bonny Island, every weld carries the mark of Olasco precision.

Our Journey

Two Decades
of Growth

From a small workshop in Rumuola to a 12,000 m² fabrication facility in Trans-Amadi — the Olasco story is one of relentless investment in Nigerian engineering capability.

2001
Company Founded

Olasco Engineering & Fabrication Ltd incorporated in Lagos. First workshop established in Rumuola with a team of 12. First contract: structural fabrication for a Shell SPDC flow station in Bayelsa.

2005
Trans-Amadi Facility

Relocated to and commissioned our current 12,000 m² workshop in Trans-Amadi Industrial Layout, Lagos. Added CNC plasma cutting, hydraulic press brakes, and sub-arc welding capability.

2008
ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification from Bureau Veritas — one of the first indigenous Nigerian fabricators to attain this standard. ASME Section IX stamp authorised.

2013
NCDMB Pioneer Status

Awarded Pioneer Fabricator status by the Nigerian Content Development & Monitoring Board. Secured first major NLNG contract — structural steel for the utility area of Bonny Island.

2018
Engineering Division Launched

Formally established in-house mechanical engineering division with 3D CAD modelling (AVEVA E3D), FEA analysis, and full engineering documentation packages. COREN-registered team of 14 professional engineers.

2024
NLNG Train 7 — Landmark Project

Completed delivery of 1,200+ tonnes of structural steel for NLNG Train 7 — the largest single fabrication contract in Olasco history. Workforce now exceeds 280 permanent staff and 140 contract artisans.
