End-to-End Capability

From Design to

Olasco offers a fully integrated fabrication and engineering service — meaning your project can go from initial scope and engineering concept all the way through to fabrication, inspection, surface treatment, and site installation support under one roof, one quality system, one accountable team at our Iganmu Industrial Estate workshop in Lagos.

01

Structural
Fabrication

Olasco fabricates heavy structural steel for onshore and offshore facilities across Nigeria. Our Lagos workshop handles everything from primary structural frames and pipe racks to access platforms, walkways, stairways, and equipment support skids.

Our steel fabrication capability covers carbon steel (A36, A572), high-strength low-alloy (HSLA), stainless, and weathering steel. All structural work is executed to AISC / AWS D1.1 and client-specific standards. We regularly supply EPC contractors and operators working on Lagos area terminals, the Apapa corridor, and offshore platforms.

Pipe racks, equipment skids, and module frames Offshore jacket structures and deck modules Jetty, pontoon, and marine berthing structures Flare stacks, derrick masts, and elevated platforms Flow station and terminal structural packages 3D model-based fabrication (AVEVA E3D)

02

Pipe Spool
Fabrication

Pipe fabrication is at the core of Olasco's workshop capability. We produce pipe spools in carbon steel, stainless steel (304/316L), duplex and super-duplex, alloy steel, and lined piping systems for all process, utility, and firewater applications.

Our Lagos pipe shop is equipped with CNC pipe cutting machines, automatic root-pass TIG welding stations, post-weld heat treatment (PWHT) furnaces, and a full hydrostatic test bay. We serve NNPC, Chevron, TotalEnergies, and major EPC contractors operating across Nigeria from our Iganmu facility.

Carbon, SS, duplex, and alloy pipe spools ½" to 60" PWHT, stress relieving, and heat treatment Hydrostatic and pneumatic pressure testing Pipe support and hanger fabrication Isometric drawing interpretation and production PCM/Weld mapping and traceability documentation
03

Pressure Vessels
& Tanks

Olasco is an ASME-authorised pressure vessel manufacturer for vessels designed and fabricated to ASME Section VIII Div. 1 and Div. 2. Our pressure vessel shop produces separators, scrubbers, slug catchers, heat exchangers, and production headers for Nigeria's upstream and processing sectors.

We also fabricate atmospheric and low-pressure storage tanks to API 650, API 620, and BS EN 14015 for crude oil, refined products, produced water, and chemical storage at terminals, tank farms, and distribution facilities across Lagos and Nigeria.

ASME VIII Div. 1 & 2 pressure vessels Two-phase and three-phase separators Shell & tube heat exchangers (TEMA) API 650 / API 620 storage tanks Slug catchers and production manifolds Witness testing and Authorised Inspector (AI) support

04

Mechanical
Engineering

Our in-house mechanical engineering team in Lagos provides the full engineering design service — from conceptual layout and 3D modelling through to detailed design, material selection, stress analysis, and complete documentation packages ready for NUPRC submission and client approval.

All engineering work is performed by COREN-registered Professional Engineers using AVEVA E3D (3D plant design), AutoCAD, CAESAR II (pipe stress analysis), PV Elite (vessel design), and STAAD.Pro (structural analysis).

3D plant and equipment modelling (AVEVA E3D) Pipe stress analysis (CAESAR II) Structural analysis and FEA (STAAD.Pro / ANSYS) Pressure vessel design (PV Elite / ASME VIII) Material take-off and procurement support NUPRC-ready engineering documentation packages
05

NDT &
Inspection

Olasco operates a fully equipped, independent NDT and inspection division based in Lagos. Our Level II and Level III certified technicians carry out non-destructive testing on all weld joints and base materials — ensuring structural integrity before any fabricated item leaves our Iganmu facility.

Our inspection services also include third-party inspection support, dimensional verification, material certification review (MTC traceability), positive material identification (PMI), hardness testing, and coating/painting inspection to applicable standards.

Ultrasonic Testing (UT) — manual and phased array (PAUT) Radiographic Testing (RT) — X-ray and gamma Magnetic Particle Testing (MT) Penetrant Testing (PT) Visual Inspection (VT) — CWI certified inspectors Positive Material Identification (PMI) and hardness

06

Modular
Construction

Modular and skid-based fabrication is increasingly the preferred delivery method for Nigerian oil & gas and industrial operators — particularly for projects where on-site construction windows are limited, or where logistics from Lagos to site require a high level of pre-commissioning at the fabrication stage.

Olasco fabricates complete process modules — fully assembled, tested, and commissioned at our Iganmu, Lagos facility before transportation to site. This reduces on-site man-hours, compresses schedules, and significantly reduces exposure to site-based HSE risks.

Wellhead, separator, and metering skids Chemical injection and dosing packages Pump and compressor skids Electrical and instrumentation (E&I) hook-up Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) facilitation Barge-ready and road-transport module packages
